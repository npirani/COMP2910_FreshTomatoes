About Fresh Tomatoes


Fresh Tomatoes is a smart gocery list that allows users to input their shopping list before a grocery store visit. It allows users to enter the expiry date of the product as they check it off the list at the grocery store.Users can refer to the list and use their items before they go bad


Fresh Tomatoes was a created by COMP 2910 students in the effort to reduce food wastage.
